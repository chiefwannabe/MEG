About Us

IGNOU Study Hub is a non-profit educational initiative created by students, for students. Our mission is simple — make quality education accessible to everyone by offering free access to Previous Year Question Papers, handwritten notes, solved assignments, and study guides.

We believe that financial barriers should never come in the way of learning. That's why every resource on this platform is completely free, with no hidden charges, no registration walls, and no restrictions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about IGNOU Study Hub.

Are the resources free?

Yes, absolutely. All PYQs, notes, assignments, and study guides on this platform are 100% free to access and download, with no hidden charges.

Is registration required?

No, you do not need to register or create an account. All resources are available instantly to everyone.

Are PYQs updated every year?

Yes, we aim to update the Previous Year Question Papers section after every exam session so you always have access to the latest papers.

Can I contribute notes?

Yes! We welcome contributions from students. If you have quality notes or solved assignments to share, please reach out to us via the contact section.
