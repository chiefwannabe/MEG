Section 02

About This Initiative

pg. 2

We are a group of IGNOU alumni and current students who believe that quality education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of financial constraints. This non-profit initiative was born out of a simple observation: too many students struggle to find reliable, free study materials for their IGNOU courses.

Our mission is to bridge that gap by curating and sharing Previous Year Question Papers, handwritten notes, solved assignments, syllabus documents, and study guides — all completely free of charge. No hidden fees, no premium tiers, no registration walls.

Every resource on this platform is contributed by students, reviewed by our volunteer team, and made available to the entire IGNOU community. We are not officially affiliated with Indira Gandhi National Open University, but we are deeply committed to supporting its students.

Section 06

Frequently Asked Questions

pg. 6
Are the resources really free?
Yes, absolutely. Every resource on this platform is 100% free. We do not charge for downloads, and there are no premium or paid tiers. Our mission is to make education accessible to all IGNOU students.
Is registration required to download materials?
No. You can browse and download all resources without creating an account. We believe in removing barriers to education, not creating them.
Are the PYQs updated every year?
Yes. We update our question paper collection after every exam session. Our volunteer team works to ensure the latest papers are uploaded within weeks of the examination.
Can I contribute my notes or papers?
Absolutely! We encourage students to contribute their notes, solved papers, and study materials. Every contribution helps the community. Reach out via our contact page to get started.
Is this website officially affiliated with IGNOU?
No. This is an independent, non-profit educational initiative. We are not officially affiliated with Indira Gandhi National Open University. We are students helping students.
How often is new content added?
We add new resources weekly. Major updates happen at the start of each semester and after examination results are declared. Follow us to stay informed about new additions.
